FAQ'S

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT TYPE OF PHOTOGRAPHY DO YOU SPECIALIZE IN? ▲
I specialize in [Portrait, Landscape, Event, etc.] photography, capturing moments that tell unique stories.
HOW CAN I BOOK A PHOTOGRAPHY SESSION WITH YOU? ▼
You can book a session by filling out the contact form on my website or sending me an email directly.
WHAT EQUIPMENT DO YOU USE FOR YOUR PHOTOGRAPHY? ▼
I use professional DSLR and mirrorless cameras along with high-quality lenses and lighting equipment.
CAN I REQUEST A SPECIFIC LOCATION FOR A SESSION? ▼
Absolutely! We can discuss and choose a location that best suits the style and theme you are looking for.
WHAT IS YOUR EDITING PROCESS LIKE? ▼
My editing process focuses on enhancing natural beauty while maintaining the authenticity of the moment.
ARE DIGITAL FILES INCLUDED IN YOUR PHOTOGRAPHY PACKAGES? ▼
Yes, high-resolution digital files are included in all my photography packages.
DO YOU OFFER PRINTS OF YOUR PHOTOGRAPHS? ▲
Yes, prints are available for purchase. Explore the 'Prints' section for more details on sizes and pricing.
HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO RECEIVE THE EDITED PHOTOS AFTER A SESSION? ▼
Turnaround time is typically 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity of the session.
